Service Applications

Occasionally referred to as laser ablation, laser cleaning is a relatively new technology with a huge number of commercial and industrial applications. In its simplest form laser cleaning is the process of removing undesired material from a solid surface by irradiating it with a pulse laser beam. Tasks such as paint removal, surface preparation, mold removal, corrosion cleaning, de-oiling and restoration of surfaces such as stone and concrete can all be completed with a laser!

By using a laser there is no need for chemical, manual, or abrasive blasting cleaning methods.
